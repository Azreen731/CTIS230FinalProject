Major Attractions

  • Ellis Island: Once a popular immigrant station, this island situated between New York and New Jersey is now home to a historical museum and is a short ferry ride away from the Statue of Liberty
  • Brooklyn Bridge: This bridge over the East River connects Brooklyn to Manhattan and is one of the icons of New York City
  • The Metropolitan Museum of Art: Known as "the Met", this art museum holds the largest collection of works in the United States

Major Attractions

  • Walt Disney World: Walt Disney World (more commonly known as Disney World) attracts tourists from all over the world for its four theme parks: Magic Kingdom, Animal Kingdom, Hollywood Studios, and Epcot
  • Universal Studios: This attraction, centered around popular movies and television series, includes three theme parks: Universal Studios, Universal Islands of Adventure, and Volcano Bay
  • International Drive: This 11 mile strip is a popular tourist destination in Orlando, housing various rides and restaurants, as well as ICON park with the Orlando Eye

Major Attractions

  • Canada National Tower: The Canada National Tower, or the CN Tower for short, is an observation tower that was once the tallest freestanding structure in the world until 2007
  • Ripley's Aquarium of Canada: This public aquarium, located at the base of the CN Tower, is one of the three aquariums operated by Ripley Entertainment
  • Canada's Wonderland: Located in the greated Toronto area, this amusement park hold over 200 attractions and a 20-acre splash park
