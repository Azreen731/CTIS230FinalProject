Cox's Bazaar, Bangladesh

Hotels/Resorts

Top Restaurants

  • Punchbowl Coffee Shop

Major Attractions

  • Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Safari Park: This safari experience and refuge allows tourists to view Bangladesh's endangered species in their natural habitat.
  • Himchori: Take a hike up a hill to a scenic cascading waterfall overlooking the beach harbor of Cox's Bazaar.
  • Laboni Beach: Cox's Bazaar hosts the longest sea beach in the world, including this one.
